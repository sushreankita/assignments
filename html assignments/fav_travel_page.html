My Favorite Travel Place

PURI

Puri is a famous city in the state of Odisha, India, known for its rich culture, history, and beautiful beaches. It is one of the four sacred Char Dham pilgrimage sites for Hindus and is home to the famous Jagannath Temple. The city is popular for its annual Rath Yatra (Chariot Festival) and attracts thousands of tourists and devotees every year.
